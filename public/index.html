Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you travel to all counties in Kenya?
+
Yes — we cover all 47 counties. Travel costs for distant locations are quoted upfront with full transparency.
How far in advance should I book?
+
For weddings, 3–6 months in advance. For corporate events, 2–4 weeks is usually sufficient. Last-minute bookings accommodated where possible.
How long before I receive my photos/videos?
+
Edited photos within 72 hours for most events. Wedding photos within 1 week. Highlight films 1–2 weeks. Full wedding films 3–4 weeks.
Are your drone pilots licensed?
+
Yes. All operators hold valid KCAA Remote Pilot Licences and we carry full public liability insurance.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
M-Pesa, bank transfer and cash. 30% deposit required to secure your date, balance due on event day.
Can I get both photos and video in one package?
+
Absolutely. Our combined packages are most popular — a single team handles both seamlessly.
